Chain Rule in Backpropagation

Understanding Chain Rule

  • 1 Composite Functions
    Neural networks are compositions of functions
  • 2 Derivative Chain
    Calculate gradients by multiplying local derivatives
  • 3 Backward Pass
    Propagate error from output to input layers
  • 4 Weight Updates
    Use accumulated gradients to update parameters

Mathematical Foundation

Chain Rule Formula:

∂L/∂w = ∂L/∂y · ∂y/∂z · ∂z/∂w

where:
L = Loss function
y = Output of layer
z = Pre-activation
w = Weight parameter
General Form:

dL/dx = Σ(∂L/∂yi · ∂yi/∂x)
