Why Dimensionality Reduction?

Key Benefits

Dimensionality Reduction transforms high-dimensional data into a lower-dimensional representation while preserving important information.
  • 📊 Data Visualization
    Project data to 2D/3D for human understanding
  • ⚡ Computational Efficiency
    Faster training and prediction with fewer features
  • 🎯 Noise Reduction
    Remove irrelevant features and focus on signal
  • 💾 Storage Savings
    Compress data while keeping information

Main Approaches

  • 🔄 Feature Extraction
    Create new features from combinations (PCA, t-SNE)
  • ✂️ Feature Selection
    Choose subset of original features
  • 🧠 Manifold Learning
    Discover underlying low-dimensional structure
  • 🤖 Deep Learning
    Autoencoders learn compressed representations
Before vs After Dimensionality Reduction
❌ Without Reduction
Dimensions: 10,000
Training Time: 2 hours
Storage: 5 GB
Visualization: Impossible
Overfitting: High Risk
✅ With Reduction
Dimensions: 50
Training Time: 2 minutes
Storage: 25 MB
Visualization: 2D/3D plots
Overfitting: Reduced
💡 Result: 95% variance retained with 0.5% of original dimensions!
