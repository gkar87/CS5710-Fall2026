📊 Layer Selection Strategy

  • Early Layers: Generic features (edges, textures)
  • Middle Layers: Shape and object parts
  • Late Layers: Task-specific features
  • Classifier: Always replace for new task

⚡ Learning Rate Strategies

  • Differential Rates: Lower for pre-trained layers
  • Warm-up: Start with very low LR
  • Cosine Annealing: Gradual reduction
  • 1cycle Policy: Cyclic learning rates

📈 Data Requirements

  • < 1K samples: Feature extraction only
  • 1K-10K: Fine-tune top layers
  • 10K-100K: Fine-tune most layers
  • > 100K: Consider training from scratch

✅ Best Practices

  • Batch Norm: Be careful with statistics
  • Data Augmentation: Essential for small datasets
  • Regularization: Dropout in new layers
  • Monitoring: Watch for overfitting
