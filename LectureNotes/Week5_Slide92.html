🏗️ Stacking (Stacked Generalization)

Core Concept

Stacking uses a meta-learner to optimally combine predictions from multiple base models, learning the best way to blend their outputs.
🎯 Key Insight:
Instead of simple voting/averaging, learn how to combine predictions intelligently.

🧠 Meta-Learning:
Train a model on the predictions of other models.

📈 Performance:
Often achieves state-of-the-art results in competitions!

Stacking Process

1
Train Base Models
Use cross-validation to train diverse Level-0 models
2
Generate Predictions
Collect out-of-fold predictions as features
3
Train Meta-Model
Learn optimal combination using Level-1 learner
4
Final Prediction
Use meta-model to blend base predictions

Training Data
→

🌟 Advantages & Considerations

✅ Superior Performance
Often beats individual models and simple voting by 1-5% accuracy
✅ Automatic Weighting
Meta-learner finds optimal combination weights automatically
⚠️ Overfitting Risk
Requires careful cross-validation to prevent data leakage
⚠️ Computational Cost
Training multiple models + meta-learner increases complexity
