📋 Industry Frameworks

MLOps (Machine Learning Operations): A set of practices that combines ML, DevOps, and Data Engineering to deploy and maintain ML systems in production reliably and efficiently.
  1. 1 Development: Experiment tracking, version control, and collaborative development
  2. 2 Training: Automated pipelines for data processing and model training
  3. 3 Deployment: Containerization, orchestration, and serving infrastructure
  4. 4 Monitoring: Performance tracking, drift detection, and automated alerts
  5. 5 Governance: Model lifecycle management, compliance, and audit trails
CRISP-DM (Cross Industry Standard Process for Data Mining): A proven methodology for structuring data science projects from business understanding to deployment.
  1. 1 Business Understanding: Define objectives, assess situation, determine goals
  2. 2 Data Understanding: Collect, describe, explore, and verify data quality
  3. 3 Data Preparation: Clean, construct, integrate, and format datasets
  4. 4 Modeling: Select techniques, build models, and assess results
  5. 5 Evaluation: Evaluate results, review process, determine next steps
  6. 6 Deployment: Plan deployment, monitor, maintain, and produce reports
Team Data Science Process (TDSP): Microsoft's agile, iterative methodology for delivering predictive analytics solutions and intelligent applications efficiently.
  1. 1 Business Understanding: Define objectives, identify data sources, and establish success criteria
  2. 2 Data Acquisition: Ingest, explore, and set up data pipeline infrastructure
  3. 3 Modeling: Feature engineering, model training, and evaluation with cross-validation
  4. 4 Deployment: Operationalize models with monitoring, customer acceptance, and handoff
  5. 5 Customer Acceptance: Validate system meets requirements and business objectives
