Real-World Regression Applications

Linear regression is one of the most widely used algorithms in industry.
From predicting house prices to optimizing supply chains, regression powers countless business decisions.
🏠

Real Estate Pricing

Zillow and others estimate property values based on location, size, features, and market trends.
ML Techniques:
• Multiple linear regression (100+ features)
• Ridge regression for regularization
• Feature engineering for location
$2.8T+ real estate market
💹

Financial Forecasting

Investment firms predict returns, risk assessment, and portfolio optimization.
ML Techniques:
• LASSO for factor selection
• Elastic Net for correlated features
• Regularization to prevent overfitting
Manages trillions in assets
📊

Sales & Demand Forecasting

Retailers predict product demand to optimize inventory and pricing.
ML Techniques:
• Polynomial regression for seasonality
• Ridge for stability across products
• Multi-level models for hierarchies
Reduces waste by 15-20%
⚕️

Healthcare Analytics

Hospitals predict patient outcomes and treatment effectiveness.
ML Techniques:
• Multiple regression for risk scoring
• LASSO for biomarker selection
• Interpretable models
Improves patient outcomes
