GAN Training Process

🔄 Adversarial Training Loop

1
Train Discriminator
Update D to better distinguish real from fake data while keeping G fixed
2
Generate Fake Samples
G creates synthetic data from random noise vectors
3
Train Generator
Update G to fool D better while keeping D fixed
4
Evaluate Progress
Check convergence and quality of generated samples
# GAN Training Algorithm
for epoch in range(num_epochs):
  for batch in dataloader:
    # Train Discriminator
    real_data = batch
    noise = torch.randn(batch_size, latent_dim)
    fake_data = generator(noise)
    d_loss = -log(D(real_data)) - log(1-D(fake_data))
    optimize_discriminator(d_loss)

    # Train Generator
    noise = torch.randn(batch_size, latent_dim)
    fake_data = generator(noise)
    g_loss = -log(D(fake_data))
    optimize_generator(g_loss)
📉 Discriminator Loss
L_D = -E[log(D(x))] - E[log(1-D(G(z)))]
Maximize probability of correct classification
📈 Generator Loss
L_G = -E[log(D(G(z)))]
Maximize probability of fooling discriminator
