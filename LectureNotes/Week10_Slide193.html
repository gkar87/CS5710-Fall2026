Text Preprocessing Pipeline

Processing Steps

1
🧹 Text Cleaning
Remove noise, HTML tags, special characters
2
✂️ Tokenization
Split text into words, subwords, or characters
3
🔡 Normalization
Lowercase, expand contractions, handle numbers
4
🛑 Stop Words
Remove common words with little semantic value
5
🌱 Stemming/Lemmatization
Reduce words to their root form
6
🔢 Vectorization
Convert text to numerical representations

Advanced Techniques

📊 N-grams
Capture sequences of words for context
🏷️ POS Tagging
Identify grammatical parts of speech
🌍 Named Entity Recognition
Extract persons, places, organizations
📝 Feature Engineering
Create domain-specific text features
🔤 Encoding Methods
Bag-of-Words, TF-IDF, Word Embeddings
🎯 Data Augmentation
Synonym replacement, back-translation
