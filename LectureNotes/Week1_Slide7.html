Key Pipeline Principles

🔄 Iterative Process
ML pipelines are cyclical - you often return to earlier steps to improve results
💎 Data Quality First
80% of ML work is data preparation - quality data leads to better models
✅ Continuous Validation
Test and validate at every step to catch issues early
