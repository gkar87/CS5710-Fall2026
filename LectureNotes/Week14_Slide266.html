Privacy Mechanisms

# ε-Differential Privacy Definition P[M(D) ∈ S] ≤ e^ε · P[M(D') ∈ S] Where: - M: Randomized mechanism - D, D': Neighboring datasets (differ by 1) - S: Any subset of outputs - ε: Privacy parameter (smaller = more private) # Laplace Mechanism f(D) + Lap(Δf/ε) Where: - Δf: Sensitivity of function f - Lap(b): Laplace distribution with scale b # Gaussian Mechanism (for (ε,δ)-DP) f(D) + N(0, σ²) σ = Δf·√(2ln(1.25/δ))/ε

Privacy Guarantees

🛡️ Plausible Deniability:

Any individual can plausibly deny being in the dataset

🔒 Post-processing Immunity:

Privacy guarantee cannot be weakened by any computation on the output
