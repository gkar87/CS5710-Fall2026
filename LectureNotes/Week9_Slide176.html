🎯 Object Detection (YOLO, R-CNN)

Beyond Classification: Finding Objects in Images
Object Detection combines classification and localization to identify what objects are in an image and where they are located.

🏛️ R-CNN Family

  • R-CNN: Region-based CNNs
  • Fast R-CNN: Faster training and inference
  • Faster R-CNN: End-to-end detection
  • Mask R-CNN: Instance segmentation

⚡ YOLO Family

  • YOLOv1: You Only Look Once
  • YOLOv2/v3: Better accuracy and speed
  • YOLOv4/v5: State-of-the-art performance
  • YOLOv8: Latest innovations
