Maximum Margin Classifier

Margin Maximization

The Maximum Margin Classifier finds the decision boundary that maximizes the distance to the closest data points from either class, creating the largest possible "safety zone."
  • 📏 Margin Definition
    Distance from decision boundary to nearest data points
  • 🎯 Optimal Boundary
    Boundary that maximizes the margin width
  • ⚖️ Generalization
    Larger margins typically lead to better generalization
  • 🔍 Support Vectors
    Critical points that define the margin boundaries
