History and Evolution of ML

1943
Neural Networks Born
McCulloch & Pitts create first mathematical model of artificial neurons
1950
Turing Test
Alan Turing proposes test for machine intelligence
1956
AI & ML Birth
Dartmouth Conference coins "Artificial Intelligence"
1957
Perceptron
Frank Rosenblatt creates first trainable neural network
1967
Nearest Neighbor
Cover & Hart develop k-NN algorithm for pattern recognition
1974-1980
First AI Winter
Limited computing power and unrealistic expectations halt progress
1986
Backpropagation
Rumelhart, Hinton & Williams revive neural networks
1995
SVM & Random Forest
Vapnik's Support Vector Machines and Breiman's ensemble methods
1997
Deep Blue
IBM's Deep Blue defeats world chess champion Garry Kasparov
2006
Deep Learning Era
Geoffrey Hinton introduces deep belief networks
2012
AlexNet Revolution
Deep CNN wins ImageNet by huge margin, sparking DL boom
2016
AlphaGo Victory
DeepMind's AlphaGo defeats world Go champion Lee Sedol
2017
Transformer Architecture
"Attention is All You Need" revolutionizes NLP
2022-2024
Generative AI Explosion
ChatGPT, DALL-E, and LLMs transform society

Three Waves of AI/ML

🌅
1950s-1970s
Symbolic AI Era
Rule-based systems, logic programming, expert systems
📊
1980s-2000s
Statistical ML Era
SVMs, decision trees, Bayesian methods, ensemble learning
🧠
2010s-Present
Deep Learning Era
CNNs, RNNs, Transformers, Foundation Models, GenAI
