📊 Choosing Number of Components

🎯 Selection Methods

1️⃣ Kaiser Criterion
Keep components with eigenvalues > 1 (for standardized data)
2️⃣ Cumulative Variance
Retain components until 85-95% of variance is explained
3️⃣ Elbow Method
Look for "elbow" in scree plot where eigenvalues level off
4️⃣ Parallel Analysis
Compare eigenvalues to those from random data
5️⃣ Cross-Validation
Choose components that minimize reconstruction error
