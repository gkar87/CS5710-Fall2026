Privacy-Preserving ML

Privacy Techniques

🌐 Federated Learning
Train models on decentralized data without sharing
🔐 Homomorphic Encryption
Compute on encrypted data without decryption
🤝 Secure Multi-party Computation
Collaborative computation without revealing inputs
📊 Differential Privacy
Add noise to protect individual privacy
🎭 Synthetic Data Generation
Create privacy-safe artificial datasets

Key Challenges

⚖️ Privacy vs Utility Trade-off

More privacy often means less model accuracy

🔄 Computational Overhead

Privacy techniques can be computationally expensive

📏 Privacy Measurement

Quantifying privacy guarantees is complex

🛡️ Attack Resistance

Defending against membership and reconstruction attacks
