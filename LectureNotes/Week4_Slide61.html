Decision Trees Introduction

What are Decision Trees?

Decision Trees are powerful supervised learning algorithms that make predictions by learning simple decision rules from data features, creating a tree-like model of decisions.
  • 🌳 Hierarchical Structure
    Breaks down complex decisions into simple yes/no questions
  • 👁️ Interpretability
    Easy to understand and visualize, mimics human decision-making
  • 🎯 Versatility
    Works for both classification and regression problems
  • 📊 Non-parametric
    No assumptions about data distribution
