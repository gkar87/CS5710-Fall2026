Model Evaluation Metrics

Why Metrics Matter

Model evaluation metrics are quantitative measures that tell us how well our machine learning model is performing. They help us compare different models and make informed decisions.
Without proper metrics, we're flying blind:

• How accurate is the model?
• What types of errors is it making?
• Is it good enough for production?
• How does it compare to alternatives?

Key Metric Categories

  • 🎯 Accuracy Metrics
    Overall correctness of predictions
  • ⚖️ Precision & Recall
    Quality vs completeness of results
  • 📊 Error Metrics
    Distance between predictions and reality
  • 📈 Business Metrics
    Real-world impact and value
Different problems need different metrics. Let's see how metrics vary by task:
🔢
Classification
Accuracy, Precision, Recall, F1-Score, AUC
📈
Regression
MSE, RMSE, MAE, R-squared
🎯
Clustering
Silhouette Score, Inertia, Calinski-Harabasz
