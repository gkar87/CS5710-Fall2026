💡 Visualization Best Practices

⚖️
Scale Your Data
Always standardize features before PCA to give equal weight
🔍
Interpret Components
Examine loadings to understand what each PC represents
📊
Show Variance
Always display % variance explained by shown components
🎯
Use Biplots
Overlay feature vectors to show variable contributions
⚠️
Check Outliers
PCA is sensitive to outliers - identify and handle them
🎨
3D Caution
3D plots can be misleading - provide interactive rotation
