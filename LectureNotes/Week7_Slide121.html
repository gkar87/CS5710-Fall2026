Curse of Dimensionality

The Problem

The Curse of Dimensionality refers to various phenomena that arise when analyzing data in high-dimensional spaces that do not occur in low-dimensional settings.
As dimensions increase, we face:

• Data sparsity - points become isolated
• Distance concentration - all points seem equally far
• Computational complexity - exponential growth
• Visualization impossibility - can't see beyond 3D

Key Effects

  • 📊 Exponential Data Requirements
    Need exponentially more data to maintain density
  • 📏 Distance Becomes Meaningless
    Euclidean distance loses discriminative power
  • 🎯 Overfitting Risk
    Models become too complex with many features
  • ⚡ Computational Explosion
    Training time increases dramatically
  • 👁️ Loss of Intuition
    Human understanding breaks down
