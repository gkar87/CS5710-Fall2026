Experience Replay

Core Concept

Experience Replay stores transitions in a buffer and samples random mini-batches for training, breaking correlations in sequential data.
The Problem:
• Sequential experiences are highly correlated
• Online learning is sample inefficient
• Rare experiences are quickly forgotten

The Solution:
Store experiences: (s, a, r, s', done)
Sample randomly for training
Experience Replay Buffer:

1. Initialize replay buffer D with capacity N
2. Store transition (s, a, r, s', done) in D
3. Sample random mini-batch from D
4. Train network on mini-batch
5. Repeat

Key Benefits

  • 🔀 Breaks Correlations
    Random sampling decorrelates sequential data
  • ♻️ Data Efficiency
    Reuse experiences multiple times
  • 📊 Stable Learning
    Smooths out learning dynamics
  • 💎 Rare Events
    Preserves and learns from rare experiences
  • ⚡ Parallel Training
    Enables offline batch training
