🔄 Transfer Learning

"Don't Start From Scratch!"
Transfer Learning leverages knowledge from pre-trained models to solve new problems faster and with less data.

🎯 Why Transfer Learning?

  • Limited Data - Train on small datasets
  • Faster Training - Hours instead of weeks
  • Better Performance - Leverage ImageNet knowledge
  • Lower Cost - Less compute required

📋 Transfer Strategies

  • Feature Extraction - Use as fixed feature extractor
  • Fine-Tuning - Adapt layers to new task
  • Layer Freezing - Selective training
  • Progressive Unfreezing - Gradual adaptation
