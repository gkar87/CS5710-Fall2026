Variational Autoencoders (VAEs)

🏗️ VAE Architecture

Encoder Network
Maps input to latent distribution parameters (μ, σ)
Latent Space
Probabilistic representation with reparameterization trick
Decoder Network
Reconstructs data from sampled latent vectors
z ~ N(μ(x), σ²(x))

🎯 Key Concepts

ELBO Loss
Evidence Lower Bound optimization objective
KL Divergence
Regularizes latent space to match prior
Reparameterization Trick
Enables backpropagation through sampling
L = -E[log p(x|z)] + KL[q(z|x) || p(z)]

⚖️ VAE vs GAN Comparison

VAE Advantages
• Stable training
• Principled probabilistic framework
• Meaningful latent space
• Can compute likelihood
GAN Advantages
• Sharper, more realistic images
• No explicit likelihood needed
• More flexible architectures
• Better for high-resolution
