Hierarchical Clustering

What is Hierarchical Clustering?

Hierarchical Clustering builds a tree of clusters, creating a nested hierarchy where clusters at each level are formed by merging or splitting clusters from the previous level.
  • 🌳 Tree Structure
    Creates a dendrogram showing relationships between clusters
  • 📊 No K Required
    Unlike K-means, doesn't need pre-specified number of clusters
  • 🔍 Multi-level Analysis
    Can examine clusters at different granularity levels
  • ⚡ Deterministic
    Always produces same result for same data
