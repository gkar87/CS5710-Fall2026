Key Concepts to Explore

1. Decision Boundary: After training, observe how the classifier creates a boundary between the two classes. Points on one side predict Class 0, points on the other predict Class 1.
2. Linear Separability: Try adding points that overlap - you'll see the classifier struggles when classes are mixed together.
3. Probability: Test points closer to the boundary have more uncertain predictions (near 50%). Points far from the boundary are more confident.
