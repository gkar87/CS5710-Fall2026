Weight Initialization Strategies

Initialization Methods

Xavier/Glorot Initialization
W ~ N(0, 2/(nin + nout))
He Initialization
W ~ N(0, 2/nin)
LeCun Initialization
W ~ N(0, 1/nin)
Uniform Initialization
W ~ U(-√(6/(nin+nout)), √(6/(nin+nout)))

Why Initialization Matters

🔥 Vanishing Gradients
Too small weights → signals die out
💥 Exploding Gradients
Too large weights → signals explode
🎯 Dead Neurons
Poor init → neurons never activate
🐌 Slow Convergence
Bad starting point → longer training
⚖️ Symmetry Breaking
Identical weights → neurons learn same

Choosing the Right Initialization

Sigmoid/Tanh
Use Xavier/Glorot
ReLU/LeakyReLU
Use He Initialization
SELU
Use LeCun Normal
