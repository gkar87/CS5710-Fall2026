PCA Algorithm Steps

Interactive Algorithm Walkthrough

1 Center the Data
Subtract the mean from each feature to center the data around the origin.
X_centered = X - μ
2 Calculate Covariance Matrix
Compute how features vary together.
C = (1/(n-1)) × X^T × X
3 Find Eigenvalues & Eigenvectors
Decompose covariance matrix to find principal directions.
C × v = λ × v
4 Sort Components
Order eigenvectors by their eigenvalues (descending).
λ₁ ≥ λ₂ ≥ ... ≥ λₙ
5 Transform Data
Project data onto selected principal components.
Y = X × W_k
