Bidirectional RNNs

Bidirectional Processing

Bidirectional RNNs process sequences in both forward and backward directions to capture complete context information.
Forward RNN
→ → →
Past to Future
+
Backward RNN
← ← ←
Future to Past
🔄 Complete Context
Access to both past and future information
🎯 Better Accuracy
Improved predictions with full sequence context
📝 Enhanced Understanding
Better comprehension of word meanings and relationships
🔍 Disambiguation
Resolves ambiguous words using surrounding context

Architecture Details

Key Components:

• Forward Layer: Processes sequence left-to-right

• Backward Layer: Processes sequence right-to-left

• Concatenation: Combines forward and backward outputs

• Output Layer: Final prediction based on combined information

Mathematical Formula:
h_t = [h_forward_t; h_backward_t]
where ; denotes concatenation
