Customer Segmentation Case Study

🏢 TechRetail Inc. - E-commerce Platform

Challenge: TechRetail Inc., a major e-commerce platform, needed to better understand their diverse customer base to improve marketing effectiveness, increase customer retention, and optimize inventory management.

Dataset Size
250,000 active customers
Time Period
24 months of transaction data
Features
15 behavioral & demographic variables
Business Goal
Increase revenue by 25%

🔍 Methodology & Approach

1
Data Collection & Preparation
Collected RFM metrics (Recency, Frequency, Monetary), demographic data, product preferences, and website behavior. Applied normalization and handled missing values.
2
Feature Engineering
Created derived features: average order value, purchase seasonality, category preferences, customer lifetime value, and engagement scores.
3
Clustering Analysis
Applied K-means clustering with k=4 (determined via elbow method and silhouette analysis). Validated results using business intuition and domain expertise.

🎯 Customer Segments Identified

👑
VIP Champions
12% (30,000)
High-value customers with frequent purchases, premium products, excellent retention. Average CLV: $2,400
💼
Loyal Professionals
31% (77,500)
Regular purchasers, tech-savvy, responsive to promotions. Moderate spending, consistent engagement. Average CLV: $890
🛍️
Bargain Hunters
38% (95,000)
Price-sensitive shoppers, seasonal buyers, promotion-driven. Lower frequency but significant volume. Average CLV: $340
🔄
At-Risk Customers
19% (47,500)
Previously active but declining engagement, potential churn risk. Require retention strategies. Average CLV: $180

💡 Key Insights & Strategic Recommendations

VIP Champions Strategy
• Exclusive early access to new products
• Dedicated customer service line
• Personalized product recommendations
• Invitation-only premium events
Loyal Professionals Approach
• Professional development content marketing
• B2B bulk purchase discounts
• Tech product focus campaigns
• Loyalty program with tier benefits
Bargain Hunters Tactics
• Aggressive promotional campaigns
• Seasonal sale notifications
• Bundle deals and volume discounts
• Flash sales and limited-time offers
At-Risk Retention Plan
• Win-back email campaigns
• Personalized discount codes
• Product recommendations based on past purchases
• Survey feedback to understand issues
