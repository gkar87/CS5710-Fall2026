AI/ML Ethics Framework

Core Ethical Principles

Responsible AI requires a comprehensive ethical framework that guides the development, deployment, and governance of machine learning systems.
  • ⚖️ Beneficence & Non-maleficence
    AI should benefit humanity and avoid harm
  • 🎯 Autonomy & Agency
    Respect human decision-making and control
  • ⚡ Justice & Fairness
    Ensure equitable treatment and outcomes
  • 🔍 Transparency & Explainability
    Make AI decisions understandable
  • ✅ Accountability & Responsibility
    Clear ownership of AI decisions and impacts

Implementation Framework

  • 📋 Ethical Impact Assessment
    Evaluate potential consequences before deployment
  • 👥 Ethics Review Board
    Multi-stakeholder oversight and governance
  • 📚 Development Guidelines
    Best practices for ethical AI development
  • 📊 Continuous Monitoring
    Ongoing assessment of deployed systems
  • 🔧 Remediation Process
    Procedures for addressing ethical issues
