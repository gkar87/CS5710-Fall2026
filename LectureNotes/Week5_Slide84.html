AdaBoost Weight Updates

Weight Update Formulas

Error Rate Calculation
ε_t = Σ(w_i × I[y_i ≠ h_t(x_i)]) / Σw_i
Weighted error of weak learner t
Learner Weight (Alpha)
α_t = 0.5 × ln((1 - ε_t) / ε_t)
Higher α for lower error rates
Sample Weight Update
w_i^(t+1) = w_i^(t) × exp(-α_t × y_i × h_t(x_i))
• Correct: y_i × h_t(x_i) = 1 → weight decreases
• Wrong: y_i × h_t(x_i) = -1 → weight increases
Normalization
w_i = w_i / Σw_j
Ensures weights sum to 1
