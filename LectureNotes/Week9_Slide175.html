🎨 Data Augmentation for Images

Creating More Training Data Without Collecting More Images
Data augmentation artificially expands your dataset by creating realistic variations of existing images.

📐 Geometric Transforms

  • Rotation: Rotate images by small angles
  • Translation: Shift images horizontally/vertically
  • Scaling: Zoom in/out of images
  • Shearing: Slant images along axes
  • Flipping: Mirror images horizontally/vertically

💡 Photometric Transforms

  • Brightness: Adjust image luminosity
  • Contrast: Modify difference between light/dark
  • Saturation: Change color intensity
  • Hue: Shift color spectrum
  • Gamma: Adjust midtone contrast
