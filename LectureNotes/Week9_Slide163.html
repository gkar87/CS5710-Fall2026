📸 Convolutional Neural Networks (CNNs)

🧠 What are CNNs?

  • 🎯 Definition
    Neural networks specialized for processing grid-like data (images)
  • 🔍 Convolution Operation
    Apply learnable filters to detect patterns and features
  • 📊 Hierarchical Learning
    Low-level features → Complex objects progressively
  • 🎲 Translation Invariance
    Detect features regardless of their position in the image
  • ⚡ Parameter Efficiency
    Shared weights reduce overfitting and computation
